Everything You Need to Improve

Not another stats dashboard. A deliberate practice tool built from the ground up.

📖

Session Journaling

Pre-session goals, per-game reflections, and post-session review. Track what you learned, not just whether you won.

🎬

Game Recording

Native screen recording with one-click start. Flag moments of confusion or frustration with a hotkey during live play for later review.

📊

Auto-Fetched Stats

Match data pulls directly from the Riot API. No manual entry. KDA, CS, vision, gold, items, runes — all there when you open your journal.

🎯

Learning Objectives

Set improvement goals and track them across sessions. Champion notes and matchup guides that grow with you.

🔍

Game-Aware

Detects when League is running, auto-fills champion and matchup data from champ select, and tracks your session flow automatically.

🛡️

In-Game Overlay

A transparent, always-on-top window showing your session goals and matchup reminders while you play. Toggle it with a hotkey.

Why Matchbook?

Every LoL companion tool is a stats dashboard. Matchbook is the first tool built around how players actually improve.

Built for Mac

Native macOS app using Swift, SwiftUI, and ScreenCaptureKit. No Electron wrappers, no web views, no Windows ports. Made for your Mac.

Reflection Over Stats

Stats tell you what happened. Journaling helps you understand why. Matchbook is designed around deliberate practice, not dashboard overload.

Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. No cloud accounts, no telemetry, no ads. Your journal, your recordings, your improvement — all local.

No In-Game Advantage

Matchbook doesn't tell you what to build or who to ban. It helps you learn from your own games through reflection and review — the way real improvement works.
